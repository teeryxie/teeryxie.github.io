Phylogenetic generative models

Phylogenetic inference is a natural place to combine structured latent variables with flexible generative modeling.

PhyloVAE studies unsupervised learning of phylogenetic trees through variational autoencoders. ARTree studies a deep autoregressive model for tree inference. Together, they form a coherent research line around probabilistic learning over tree-structured scientific data.
