Flow matching notes

Flow matching gives a direct way to learn generative vector fields; structure and constraints make the setting more interesting.

Reflected Flow Matching and Constraint-Aware Flow Matching focus on making flow-based generative learning work with structured domains and explicit constraints. This line is complementary to variational inference and discrete diffusion work.
