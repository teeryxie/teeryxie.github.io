Long-video frame selection

Long-video understanding needs better frame budgets, not just larger context windows.

Event-Anchored Frame Selection and WFS-SB both reduce redundancy by choosing representative video frames with event or semantic-boundary structure. The site links both the OpenReview submission and arXiv/code artifacts where available.
