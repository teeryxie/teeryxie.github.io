What SocialOmni measures

Social interaction for omni-modal models cannot be reduced to static answer accuracy.

SocialOmni decomposes conversational interactivity into three coupled questions: who is speaking, when a model should enter the conversation, and how it should phrase an interruption. This framing makes it easier to diagnose whether failures come from perception, timing, or generation.
