Research Themes

◉ Omni-modal evaluation

Benchmarks that measure not only recognition accuracy, but also interactive behavior: who is speaking, when to enter, and how to respond.

◇ Training-free orchestration

Modular systems that coordinate off-the-shelf modality experts through explicit LLM routing, memory, and streaming interaction.

▤ Long-video understanding

Event-aware and semantic-boundary-aware frame selection for reducing redundancy while preserving narrative structure.

□ Reasoning benchmarks

Formal and scalable evaluation settings for abstract reasoning, visual latent reasoning, and model generalization.
