I work on multimodal large language models, long-video understanding, efficient omni-modal orchestration, and benchmark construction for interactive AI systems. My current research focuses on training-free omni-modal systems, audio-visual social interaction benchmarks, and efficient frame selection for long-video reasoning.

Research Themes

★ Multimodal orchestration

Training-free systems that coordinate modality experts through LLM routing, persistent memory, and streaming interaction.

▣ Long-video understanding

Event-aware and semantic-boundary-aware frame selection for efficient long-video reasoning.

⚡ Efficient inference

Speculative decoding and online drafting methods that accelerate large language model generation.

🧩 Mixture-of-experts

Routing-guided expert selection and gradient-interference mitigation for mixture-of-experts models.
